Mental Health Support for Undergraduate Students: Dipo Mindwell

I was lucky this time to be selected as UNESCO Youth As Researcher - Tanoto Student Research Awards (YAR-TSRA) participant. This program was a training and mentoring program to support young people to design and conduct social research under the auspices of Unesco and the Tanoto Foundation. From the offered theme, I choose Digital Transformation with idea for Enhancing Mental Health Support in Diponegoro University via Digital Platform.

With the opportunity from the research, I decide to make a prototype for the web. Anyway, did you know that in unversity environment, there is a lack of awareness regarding students mental health? There is no avaiable tool or service that is adequate enough to hold the student's mental health meeds. Also, majority tends to keep their conditions quiet or not seeking help to experts. It's also the reason I choose this topic for my social research.

Through literature review, web prototype development, and survey from 35 Diponegoro University Students in various Faculty. My team gain some insight and conclusion that I present in Poster Format below.


I also decided to name the research and web Dipo Mindwell. Dipo refer for the name of my University, "Diponegoro". Mindwell have a mean that quite literal, have a mind that healty and well enough for productive study.
